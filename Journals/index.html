J.R. Carpenter || A Handmade Web
Nowadays, websites are automated or built from templates.
- it is more like a website that is limited or inhibited by the web as we know it today

"I evoke the term 'handmade web' to refer to web pages coded by hand rather than by software; web pages made and maintained by individuals rather than by businesses or corporations;
web pages which are provisional, temporary, or one-of-a-kind; web pages which challenge conventions of reading, writing, design, ownership, privacy, security, or identity."

After reviewing / visiting the "Fishes & Flying things" website, the format of the web was totally different than other websites.
- diy aesthetics="personal"
- site as a personal space
A Rant About "Technology"
Author is arguing that technology is not just wires & servers but also "the active human interface with the materials world." Perspective of "technology" seems to change depending on the development of the world & the atmosphere of the society.
- usually thought about phone, semiconductor as a technology
- grouped hi-tech & tech as a same thing

After reading this, I've come to think that tech is what I can do now that I couldn't do before.
"I don't know how to build and power a refrigerator, or program a computer, but I don't know how to make a fishhook or a pair of shoes, either. I could learn. We all can learn. That's the neat thing about technologies. They're what we can learn to do."
- hi tech ≠ tech
- tech is much broader than definition of the hi-tech
- tech = easily emerging / affecting in our daily lives
- practice on thinking differently than what all people think (think outside of the box)
